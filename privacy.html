Lia Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-11-04

At Lia, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic contact details (name, email, phone) provided voluntarily.
  • Usage data for improving app performance.
  • No sensitive personal information is collected unless required for authentication.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate and improve Lia’s real estate marketing automation tools.
  • To provide user support and service updates.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • Access is restricted to authorized systems only.
  • Users may request data deletion at any time.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell or share user data with third parties.
  • Data may be processed by integrated APIs (Google, LinkedIn, etc.) with explicit user consent.

5. Your Rights

  • You may access, correct, or delete your data at any time.
  • Requests can be sent to support@propertiesbyettienne.co.za.

6. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page.
